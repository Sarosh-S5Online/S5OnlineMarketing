Frequently asked questions

Still have questions? Here are the answers.

Question not listed? Just send us a message.

What does a website cost at S5Online Marketing?

Every project is bespoke, so it depends on what you need. After a short strategy call you get a clear, fixed price before we start. No surprises afterwards.

What is the minimum budget for Google or Meta Ads?

We recommend a minimum ad budget of 500 to 1,000 euros per month for noticeable results. Our management fee is separate. We are always transparent about what you pay and what you get for it.

How long before I see results from SEO?

SEO is a longer-term investment. You often see the first improvements within 4 to 8 weeks, but real dominance in your market is built over 3 to 6 months. You get a clear report every month so you know exactly where you stand.

Am I tied into anything?

No. We work month to month. We earn your trust every month with results, not with contracts. You can stop whenever you want.

Do you work with businesses outside Heerlen?

Absolutely. We are based in Heerlen but work with businesses across the Netherlands and beyond. Digital marketing knows no borders, and neither do we.

How does the free strategy call work?

A 30-minute call, straight with Sarosh. No sales pitch, no pressure. We talk through your business and your goals. You leave with honest advice, whether you work with us or not.

Can I make changes to my website myself?

Yes. After launch you can edit text and photos yourself. For bigger changes or new pages you can always come to us.
